
# Planetary Transits

Planetary transits occur when the current positions of planets interact with positions in a natal chart. Astrologers use transits to understand current themes, opportunities, and challenges.

Transits do not force events to happen. Instead, they are often viewed as symbolic indicators of changing energies and developmental cycles.

# Jupiter Transits

Jupiter transits often correspond with growth, learning, expansion, optimism, and opportunity. These periods may encourage exploration, education, travel, or personal development.

# Saturn Transits

Saturn transits often bring focus to responsibility, commitment, structure, and long-term planning. They may require patience and maturity but frequently lead to lasting growth.

# Mars Transits

Mars transits increase energy, initiative, courage, and motivation. They may inspire action and progress, though they can also increase impatience or conflict if not managed carefully.

# Venus Transits

Venus transits highlight relationships, harmony, pleasure, beauty, and social connection. These periods often encourage cooperation and appreciation.

# Mercury Transits

Mercury transits emphasize communication, learning, planning, travel, and information exchange. They often bring increased mental activity and conversation.

# Long-Term Perspective

Transits are most useful when viewed as opportunities for reflection rather than predictions. They can help individuals understand evolving themes in their lives and make more conscious decisions.
