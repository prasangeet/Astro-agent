
# First House

The First House represents identity, personality, self-image, appearance, and how an individual approaches life. It is closely connected to the Ascendant and often influences first impressions.

# Second House

The Second House governs finances, possessions, personal values, self-worth, and material resources. It reflects how an individual seeks stability and security.

# Third House

The Third House represents communication, learning, siblings, short journeys, and the exchange of information. It reflects curiosity and intellectual engagement.

# Fourth House

The Fourth House governs home, family, roots, ancestry, emotional foundations, and private life. It often describes one's relationship with belonging and security.

# Fifth House

The Fifth House is associated with creativity, joy, romance, self-expression, entertainment, and children. It represents the desire to create and enjoy life.

# Sixth House

The Sixth House governs work, health, service, habits, discipline, and daily responsibilities. It often reflects how a person organizes life and maintains wellbeing.

# Seventh House

The Seventh House represents partnerships, marriage, contracts, collaboration, and close relationships. It highlights how a person relates to others on an equal basis.

# Eighth House

The Eighth House is associated with transformation, intimacy, shared resources, psychological depth, inheritance, and profound life changes.

# Ninth House

The Ninth House governs philosophy, higher education, spirituality, long-distance travel, exploration, and the search for meaning.

# Tenth House

The Tenth House represents career, public image, reputation, leadership, achievement, and long-term ambitions. It often reveals how a person seeks recognition and contributes to society.

# Eleventh House

The Eleventh House governs friendships, communities, social networks, collective goals, innovation, and future aspirations.

# Twelfth House

The Twelfth House is associated with spirituality, solitude, reflection, hidden patterns, healing, dreams, and the unconscious mind.
